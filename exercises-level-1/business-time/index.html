ABOUT

My name is Jon William. I am a concert photographer currently based out of Tampa Bay, FL. A drummer and avid music lover, I developed an interest in concert photography out of a desire to:

  • Interact with musicians
  • Support local music scenes
  • Assist artists/bands establish a flourishing internet presence by enhancing their content

As a photographer, my goal is to provide an immersive experience for all. I strive to capture the raw magic of every performance by highlighting all musicians thriving in their element.
